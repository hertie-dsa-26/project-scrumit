About FRISK - Financial Risk Assessment

FRISK is designed for banking professionals, compliance officers, and financial analysts who need to know one thing fast: is this transaction clean?

Whether you're investigating a flagged payment, exploring trends across thousands of transactions, or just trying to get a clearer picture of where suspicious activity clusters, FRISK puts the tools in your hands. Run a transaction through our ML model for an instant laundering risk prediction, or dive into our dashboards to explore patterns, anomalies, and network relationships across the data.

No data science background required. Just bring your suspicions.
